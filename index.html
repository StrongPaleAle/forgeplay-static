About

We are a team of video game enthusiasts unsatisfied with the usual entertainment for its own sake.
We believe that games can beneficially impact people's lives, and we want to prove it with our projects.
Our team has a clear mission: create games that stimulate critical thinking, creativity, and deliver important messages to inspire players.
We do not make simple pastimes. We want, instead, to develop games that bear a specific purpose, a tool for the common good.
Our experience and expertise in design, game development, and storytelling allow us to combine business with pleasure, creating immersive and innovative gaming experiences.
